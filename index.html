Graduating Senior Policy

Instructors with graduating seniors enrolled in their courses are required to administer final examinations within the designated graduating senior examination window to ensure timely grade processing for commencement clearance.

Senior Exam WindowApril 23 – 24, 2026
Grade Submission DeadlineMonday, April 27, 2026 · 3:00 PM

Late grade submissions will delay commencement clearance and diploma processing. Contact the Office of the Registrar to identify graduating seniors in your course.

Examination Delivery Policy

Online Courses: Final examinations for fully online courses must be administered online within the designated final examination time frame. Online finals may not be moved outside of the examination period.

In-Person Courses: Final examinations must be held in-person during the assigned date, time, and location listed in this schedule. Instructors may not convert an in-person final to an online or take-home format without prior written approval from the Division of Academic Affairs.

Deviations from this policy require advance written approval from the Office of the Provost. Failure to adhere to the assigned examination modality and time slot may result in administrative action.
